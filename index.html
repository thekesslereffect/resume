About Me

I'm a 3D artist located in San Diego, California, working in games and film. I have worked on more than 10 AAA games and several short films over the past 5 years. While I mainly worked on characters, I have touched almost all aspects of the 3D asset pipeline. Photogrammetry, hardsurface, PBR texturing, blendshapes, and retopology just to name a few.

Whatever your project needs, I'm ready to rock.

What I Do

Modeling

Anything from hardsurface to retopology and UVs.

Photogrammetry

Processing with Reality Capture or MetaShape, and cleanup with ZBrush.

Sculpting

Organic sculpting using ZBrush as my weapon of choice.

Texturing

PBR texturing using Substance Painter, Photoshop, and Mudbox.

Grooming

Maya's XGen, Coiffure, and Yeti for hair creation. The future looks bright with the new hair system in Unreal.

Scripting

While I'm not a programmer, I find it fun to create basic scripts in Maya and ZBrush to automate my tasks.
